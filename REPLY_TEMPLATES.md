# Reply Templates

## Good fit: invite to consultation

```text
Hi <name>,

This looks like something I may be able to help with.

Let's use the free 15-minute consultation to define the real blocker and decide whether a paid session makes sense.

Please send me your time zone and 2-3 time windows that work for you.

Rustam
```

## Need more context

```text
Hi <name>,

I need a bit more context before I can say whether I can help.

Please send:

1. What are you trying to achieve?
2. What is blocked right now?
3. What tool/system/project are you working with?
4. Screenshot, short video, or link if it helps explain the situation.

Rustam
```

## Not a fit

```text
Hi <name>,

Thanks for the request.

I do not think I am the right person for this specific problem, so I do not want to sell you a session that is unlikely to be useful.

The shortest next step I would suggest is:

<one useful suggestion>

Rustam
```
